ABOUT INDRANEEL C S

Yes, One full page all about me - by me!

Indraneel C S , CS doesn't stand for counter strike; neither does it stand for Carcinogenic Syndrome. It is Chaaka Sai indeed.

A little less than 24 years is what it took me to write this. Back when I was a kid, I remember my math teacher calling me a BAD boy (just for fun though). She looked so beautiful and so I decided to make that true. So, I picked up being bad, but added MINTON to it. Years later, I played BAD-MINTON professionally!

I have always been in love with the alphabets, C and S. Creative sciences was my favourite subject, TCS, my first work place and Counter Strike, my favourite game. A-Z is what they say. But for me, It's C-S; Challenges - Success.

Back when I was a kid, people quoted me to be lazy. And, I guess they were right. But, It did one good thing for me. It made me creative and analytical. I'll tell you how. Whenever someone is lazy, they would be short of time to complete a pile of tasks. All that you need to do is think of the best possible way to get them done. Over a period of time, this became a practice and it made me a better person. As they say, there's an end to everything and even my laziness came to an end. Now, I don't need laziness to help me be creative.
